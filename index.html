All Hire a newcomer as an intern A government of Canada platform for intending government bodies to hire newcomers as interns.
All Bridging to Employment (B2E) Program A 20-week program exclusively designed for newcomers who possess valuable experience and education acquired outside of Canada with 10 weeks, of training classes and 10 weeks of work placements.
Youths (18 - 30) Learning Interculturalism Through Employment (LITE) Teaches employment skills and intercultural awareness to Indigenous and Newcomer youth (ages 18-30) in Saskatoon with 8 weeks of classes and 12 weeks of work placement.
Women in STEM, Human Resources, Finance, Accounting, Business Administration Professional Advancement and Career Training (PACT) A free work preparation program for Newcomer Women in Canada, with eight-week part-time classes tailored to individual participants’ needs as much as possible and a 12-week paid work placement with an employer in the client’s area of training.
Professionals CareerNet Program A free employment program that provides training, mentoring, and career development to newcomers looking to establish a career in Canada comprising 8-week part-time online training session and an 8 to 12-week mentoring session with a suitable mentor in the professional field to enhance connections, advice on job search, and build a professional network.
